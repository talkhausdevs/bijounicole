About Bijou Nicole

Bijou Nicole is making a significant impact in the music scene with her highly anticipated debut set for April 2024. Her first single, "Heartache Melody," has already captured the attention of listeners, amassing thousands of streams across various platforms and quickly gaining popularity on TikTok.

With its captivating lyrics and infectious melody, "Heartache Melody" showcases Bijou's unique sound and emotional depth. In addition, Bijou Nicole has released her new EP titled "Fall All Over," now available on all major streaming platforms.
